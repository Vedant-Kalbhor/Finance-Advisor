project idea **Generative Personal Finance Advisor** 

---

# 1. Core Vision of the Project

A **Generative AI-powered system** that analyzes a user's financial data and provides:

* Personalized financial planning
* Explainable recommendations
* Budgeting, investing, saving, debt, and tax guidance
* Conversational AI assistant for finance questions

---

# 2. Features (Complete, Industry-Level)

## A. User Profile & Financial Data Management

**Purpose:** Build financial context

Features:

* User registration & authentication (JWT / OAuth)
* Financial profile creation:

  * Income sources
  * Expenses
  * Savings
  * Investments
  * Debts / loans
  * Financial goals (retirement, house, car, emergency fund)

Advanced:

* Upload bank statements (PDF parsing)
* Connect bank APIs (optional future: Plaid, Open Banking)
* Manual or automatic expense tracking

---

## B. Smart Expense Tracking & Categorization

**AI Use:** NLP + classification

Features:

* Expense categorization:

  * Food
  * Rent
  * Travel
  * Entertainment
  * Bills
  * Investments

AI Features:

* Auto-categorize transactions using ML
* Detect unnecessary expenses
* Monthly spending insights

Example Output:

> You are spending 32% more on food compared to similar income profiles.

---

## C. Personalized Budget Generation (Core Feature)

**AI generates optimal budget**

Input:

* Income
* Expenses
* Goals
* Location
* Risk tolerance

Output:

* Monthly budget plan
* Recommended savings %
* Recommended investment %

Example:

```
Income: ₹60,000

Recommended:
Needs: ₹30,000 (50%)
Wants: ₹15,000 (25%)
Savings: ₹15,000 (25%)
```

Explainable AI output:

> Based on your income and goal to buy a house in 5 years, increasing savings to 25% is recommended.

---

## D. Goal-Based Financial Planning

Users define goals:

* Retirement
* House
* Car
* Emergency fund
* Education

AI calculates:

* Required monthly savings
* Expected timeline
* Optimal investment strategy

Example:

```
Goal: ₹10 lakh in 5 years

Recommendation:
Invest ₹13,200 per month in mutual funds with 12% expected return.
```

---

**AI suggests investments based on risk profile**

Risk Profiles:

* Conservative

* Aggressive

Recommendations:

* Fixed deposits
* Mutual funds

* ETFs

Advanced:

* Portfolio allocation optimization
* Diversification recommendations

Example:

```
Recommended Portfolio:

```

Core conversational interface

Users can ask:

* Can I afford a car?

* How much should I invest?
* Why is this recommended?

Powered by:
